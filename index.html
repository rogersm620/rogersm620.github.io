The Chisholm Trail (1873)

This interactive map was created to let users experience the most complete map of the Chisholm Cattle Trail available. Users can look at trail names, starting, and ending locations (when available) by hovering the cursor over trails.

This map was created by georeferencing the map The Old Chisholm Cattle Trail with Subsidiary Trails in Texas 1873 in QGIS, then digitizing individual trails. Data was then uploaded to CartoDB, and used to produce the final map.

  • Full map: The Chisholm Trail (1873)
  • Data Source: The Old Chisholm Cattle Trail with Subsidiary Trails in Texas 1873

U.S. Electric Power Generation from Non-coal Sources

This interactive map uses proportional symbology to represent non-coal sources of electricity in the United States. Users can filter their searches by three different source types, or by clicking on a location. A pop up window will display the site name, source type, and location from the selected point.

This map was created using the Leaflet JavaScript library

  • Full map: U.S. Electric Power Generation from Non-coal Sources
  • Data Source: U.S. Energy Information Administration
